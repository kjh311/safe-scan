# Design System: The Clinical Sentinel (Safe Scan) - 2026 Audit

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
The Creative North Star remains **"The Clinical Sentinel,"** but the aesthetic has been refined for higher precision and professional diagnostic clarity. 

## 2. Updated Color Palette
- **Primary (Precision Blue):** `#00B2FF`. Replaces all previous green/lime accents for a more surgical and technical feel.
- **Neutral Base (Deep Charcoal):** `#121212`. The foundational background for all screens.
- **Overlay Glassmorphism:** 80% opacity with a high-sigma backdrop blur for floaters and islands.

## 3. Surface Hierarchy
- **Base Layer:** `#121212` (Surface).
- **Overlays:** `#121212` with 80% opacity + Backdrop Blur (Glassmorphism).

## 4. Typography
- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk.
- **Body/Labels:** Inter.

## 5. UI Components (Revised)

### Floating Island Navigation
- **Layout:** Wide-spaced layout.
- **Spacing:** **48dp** between icons.
- **Background:** Glassmorphic deep charcoal.

### Scanner Viewfinder
- **Markers:** Four **L-bracket corner markers** only. 
- **Style:** No closed boxes; purely defined by the corners in Precision Blue (#00B2FF).

### Diagnostic Cards & Results
- **Visuals:** Strict adherence to high-fidelity mockups.
- **Spacing:** Vertical white space and tonal layering define data points.
